剑桥中文戏剧社是CUCCS下属的社团，并从CUCCS得到经费以每年排演两部戏(中文台词，英文字幕)。
我们为在充满历史人文气息的英国剑桥推广中文戏剧而不断努力。

The CUCCS Drama Club (CDC) is a subcommittee of the CU Chinese Cultural Society, aiming to promote Chinese theatre in the historic town of Cambridge, UK. We receive funding from the CUCCS to produce two plays a year, both in Mandarin Chinese, with English subtitles.

赞助 Sponsorship

我们提供战略合作机会，但合作者必须首先与CUCCS达成合作协议。请联系我们的制片人以了解详细信息。
We offer strategic partnerships. The partnership however needs to be a collaboration with CUCCS. Please contact CDC producer who will direct subsequent collaboration process.
